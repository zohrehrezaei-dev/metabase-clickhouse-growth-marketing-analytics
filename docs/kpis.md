In this project, I want to explore some E-commerce KPIs (each with a short definition, formula, and why it matters).
First of all, we should know the steps of *conversion funnel*: 
Impressions ---> Clicks ---> Sessions ---> Ass to card ---> Purchase

# Conversion Rate (CR)
Formula: purchases/sessions
Insight: Measures the efficiency of the website in converting visitors into buyers.
